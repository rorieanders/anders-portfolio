ACADEMIC ENDEAVORS

WHAT I'VE LEARNED


I graduated from St. Olaf College in 2013 with a B.A. in Psychology and a concentration in Family Studies. In 2018, I obtained my Master of Social Work degree at the University of Minnesota as a Title IV-E Scholar focusing on Child Welfare for families and children. In November 2019, I completed the University of Minnesota's certification program, Instructional Design for eLearning to further my interest in the field of Learning and Development. With a burgeoning affinity to technical writing and graphic design, I am persuing my second Master's degree in Scientific and Technical Communication at the University of Minnesota.
